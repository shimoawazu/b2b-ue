## Design Principles Applied

- **Restraint:** Dark navy + white + single gold accent ‚Äî no competing colors
- **Technical credibility:** Precise numbers (0.05 ¬µm, ISO 17025, 98.7%) communicate engineering authority
- **Visual hierarchy:** Inter Tight 700 for headings, Inter 400 for body ‚Äî consistent across all sections
- **Gold as action signal:** Used only on CTAs, key stats, and section eyebrows ‚Äî never decorative noise
- **Section rhythm:** Light (`#f5f7fa`) ‚Üí Dark (`#003087`) ‚Üí Light ‚Üí Dark (`#001840`) creates alternating contrast
