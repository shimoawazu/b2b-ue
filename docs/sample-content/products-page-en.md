### 2. Optical Profilometers

**Category badge:** SURFACE METROLOGY  
**Product line:** DataPilot SP Series

**Headline:** Nanometer Surface Detail. No Contact, No Contamination.

**Description:**  
When Ra values and Rz profiles need to be measured without touching the part, DataPilot SP profilometers deliver. Confocal and white-light interferometry models cover vertical resolution from 0.1 nm to surface form measurement across 100 mm scan areas — critical for semiconductor wafers, precision optics, and orthopaedic implants.

Every DataPilot SP unit ships with ISO 25178 and ASME B46.1 compliant reporting templates. Automated GD&T callout extraction from CAD nominal data reduces operator setup time from hours to minutes.

**Key specifications:**
- Vertical resolution: 0.1 nm (confocal mode)
- Scan area: 0.3 mm × 0.3 mm up to 100 mm × 100 mm
- Standards: ISO 25178, ASME B46.1, EUR 15178N
