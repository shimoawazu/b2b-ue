Lumina Noventis Named to 2026 Industry 4.0 Leaders Index
January 14, 2026 · Company · 4 min read

Lumina Noventis has been named to the 2026 Industry 4.0 Leaders Index for the third consecutive year, recognising companies that have made measurable contributions to the digital transformation of precision manufacturing.

The Index, published annually by the Advanced Manufacturing Research Centre (AMRC) in partnership with McKinsey & Company, evaluates over 1,200 technology suppliers across six dimensions: connectivity, data intelligence, automation, customer impact, sustainability integration, and workforce enablement.

Recognition Highlights
Top-50 ranking in the Connectivity and Data Intelligence categories
Recognised for OPC-UA native integration across the SpectraLink and DataPilot product families
Called out specifically for the IIoT-ready calibration workflow released in Q3 2025
“Lumina Noventis continues to close the gap between shop-floor metrology and enterprise data infrastructure. Their approach to real-time measurement traceability is setting the pace for the industry.” — AMRC Index Report, 2026

The full Index report is available for download at the AMRC website. Lumina Noventis customers can access our Industry 4.0 integration guide through the customer portal.
